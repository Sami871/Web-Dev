Fairy Meadows is a picturesque
remote location situated in northern Pakistan,
near the base of Nanga Parbat,
the ninth-highest mountain in the world.

Fairy Meadows is celebrated for its tranquil and
stunning environment, making it a favorite destination
for trekkers and nature lovers seeking an escape
into one of Pakistan’s most beautiful natural settings.
